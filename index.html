MARS ANALOG
TESTING INFRASTRUCTURE
— INDIA

Stella Martis builds and operates environments that replicate conditions on the Martian surface. Space hardware — rovers, drones, sensors, communication systems, autonomy software — is tested here before deployment.

We operate three facilities in northern India. One indoor laboratory outside Chandigarh. One outdoor terrain range in Morni Hills. One high-altitude field station in Spiti Valley. We also design and build Mars-ready hardware of our own, tested through the same pipeline.

WHAT WE DO

1. WE TEST OTHER PEOPLE'S HARDWARE

Research institutions, aerospace companies, university programs, and government agencies bring their technology to our facilities. We run it through controlled simulations — low pressure, UV radiation, regolith interaction, signal delay — and document what happens.

Outcome: Clients leave with test data and a technical report.

2. WE BUILD OUR OWN HARDWARE

We design Mars surface technology — sensors, enclosures, mobility systems, communication tools — and validate them in our own facilities before offering them for mission use.
